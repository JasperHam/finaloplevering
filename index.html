Cartoons

As a side hobby I convert real photo's into cartoon for people who ask me to do so. I learned how to this around the age of 13 and started developing this skill in the years to come. The process involves someone choosing a photo of themselves in high quality and sending it to me. I will then use Photoshop to first figure out what the main outlines are. I will then choose the main colours needed to give life to the image after which the shadows will be placed to make it look as realistic as a cartoon can.
